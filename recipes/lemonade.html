Easy Lemonade

When life gives you lemons, make Lemonade

Prep Time: 10 minutes Total Time: 10 minutes Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 2 to 3 cups cold water, to dilute

Directions

  1. Place sugar and water in a small saucepan and bring to a simmer. Stir so that the sugar dissolves then remove from heat
  2. While the water is heating, juice the lemons. Enough for 1 cup of juice
  3. Pour the juice and the syrup water into a serving pitcher. Add 2 to 3 cups of cold water and taste. Add more if you would like to be more diluted.
  4. Chill and Serve
