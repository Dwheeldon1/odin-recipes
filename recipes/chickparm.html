Chicken Parmesan

Amazingly simple and delicious

Prep Time: 30 mins Cook Time: 40 mins Total Time: 1hr 10 mins

Ingredients

    Tomato Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 medium garlic cloves, peeled and crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • teaspoon sugar
  • salt and fresh ground black pepper to taste

    • Pasta:

  • 8 ounces spaghetti or linguine

    • Chicken Cutlets:

  • 2 large boneless, skinless chicken breasts, halved crosswise
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
  • fresh ground pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup grated part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese, plus more for serving

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Make tomato sauce: Heat oil and garlic in a large saucepan over medium-high heat until garlic starts to sizzle. Stir in tomatoes, basil, oregano, and sugar; season with salt and pepper. Brong to a simmer; cook until sauce slightly thickens and flavors meld, 10 to 12 minutes. Cover and keep warm.

  2. Step 2

    3. Cook pasta: Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook sphagettin in boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes. Drain and keep warm.

  3. Step 3

    4. Make chicken cutlets: Lay chicken pieces between 2 sheets of plastic wrap. Use a mallet or heavy pan to pound chicken to about 1/4-inch thickness.

  4. Step 4

    5. Beat egg in a shallow dish until well blended. Season bread crumbs with black pepper in another shallow dish

  5. Step 5

    6. Preheat the oven broiler to high. Place a rack 4 to 6 inches from the heat source.

  6. Step 6

    7. Woking with one cutlet at a time, dip chicken in beaten egg, then in bread crumbs. Lay breaded cutlets on a wire rack set over a cookie sheet.

  7. Step 7

    8. Heat 1/4 cup oil in a 12-inch skillet over medium-high heat until it shimmers.

  8. Step 8

    9. Fry breaded cutlets in hot oil until golden brown on each side, about 5 minutes total. Wash and dry the wire rack and return to the cookie sheet. Place fried cutlets on the clean wire rack.

  9. Step 9

    10. Top each cutlet with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Broil until chesse melts and is spotty brown, 2 to 3 minutes.

  10. Step 10

    11. Portion the pasta onto 4 plates and top each with a chicken cutlet. Spoon 2 to 3 tablespoons tomato sauce over each cutlet, then sauce pasta as desired. Serve with extra Parmesan at the table
