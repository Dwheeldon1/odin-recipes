Three Ingredient Peanut Butter Cookies

Easy to Make, and Tastes Great!

Prep Time: 20 minutes Cook Time: 10 minutes Yields 1 dozen

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Peanut Butter
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1 Egg

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Mix ingredients in a medium bowl. Roll into 1 inch balls and place on parchment paper. Flatten with a fork.
  3. Bake in preheated oven until cookies are barley brown on the bottom about 6 to 8 minutes.
  4. Remove from oven and allow to cool.
